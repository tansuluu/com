What is a contact hour?

A contact hour includes any lecture or lab time when the professor is teaching the student, and they usually equal 40 minutes in real time.

What do credits mean in university?

An academic credit system is a standard used by universities to measure and assess students' work and effort during their Bachelor, Master or PhD programme. ...
